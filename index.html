Hello, and welcome to my various scripting projects

First, a userscript to download CSV files of roll calls from both the House and Senate websites. This is helpful if you're trying to download a just recently published congressional roll call vote that has not yet been published on Voteview.

Second, a userscript to see likes and dislikes on YouTube videos shown in the sidebar of the YouTube website

Third, the flag of the European Union, with stars positioned and resized according to the importance of the twelve most important E.U. countries, by several measures.
